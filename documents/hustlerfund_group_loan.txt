Are you ready to grow your business and achieve your dreams? The Group Loan Product by the Hustler Fund is here to help you and your friends access the money you need to boost your economic activity. Whether you want to start a new venture, expand your current business, or invest in new opportunities, our Group Loan Product makes it easy and affordable. What is the Group Loan Product? The Group Loan Product allows you and up to nine and above members to borrow together as a group. By joining forces, you can access loans of upto 1,000,000 . This collective approach not only makes borrowing easier but also builds a support system that helps everyone stay on track with repayments. How Does It Work? Form Your Group: If you have a group of 10 Members and above: Register the group through Micro and Small Eterprises Authority (MSEA) or Business Registration Service (BRS). Note; MSEA registration can be done through *254# under group loan. Assign Roles: Clearly define each person&rsquo;s responsibilities to ensure smooth group operations and accountability. Apply for a Loan: Submit Together: As a group, you&rsquo;ll submit a loan application through our easy digital platform or by dialing *USSD code *254#. Approval Process: Five random group members will receive an SMS to approve the loan, ensuring everyone agrees and supports the decision. Receive Funds: Disbursement: Once approved, 95% of the loan amount is sent to your group&rsquo;s mobile money wallet, and 5% is automatically saved in your group&rsquo;s savings account. Use Your Funds: Use the money to grow your business, buy equipment, stock inventory, or for other productive purposes. Repay with Ease: Flexible Terms: Repay your loan within 30 days with the option to make full or partial payments that fit your cash flow.
