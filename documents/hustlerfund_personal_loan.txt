Downloads Policies Contact Us FAQs Search Personal Loan Welcome to the Hustler Fund Personal Loan Product The Hustler Fund Personal Loan is a quick, easy, and affordable way for everyday Kenyans to access cash without collateral, helping them grow their businesses, handle emergencies, and even save a little while borrowing. Ever found yourself in need of a quick loan but stuck because banks demand too much paperwork or collateral?. That&rsquo;s exactly where the Hustler Fund Personal Loan comes in! It&rsquo;s a simple, fast, and affordable loan designed for everyday Kenyans&mdash;from small business owners to boda boda riders, mama mbogas, and anyone who needs a financial boost. Let&rsquo;s break it down in simple terms. What’s the Hustler Fund Personal Loan All About? This loan is designed to help hustlers and small business owners access money when they need it most. Whether it&rsquo;s stocking up your shop, fixing your motorbike, or handling an emergency, this loan gives you quick cash without collateral or long approval processes. It&rsquo;s all about empowering Kenyans who might struggle to get loans from traditional banks. Plus, it helps you build a credit history and even save for the future! How Much Can You Get? Anywhere between KES 100 and KES 50,000, depending on your credit score and repayment history. A super-friendly 8% per year (calculated daily). You have 14 days to pay back the loan. Every time you borrow, 5% of the loan amount goes straight into your savings, so you&rsquo;re growing your money while borrowing! You don&rsquo;t have to pledge your land, car, or business&mdash;this loan is unsecured, meaning it&rsquo;s based on your trustworthiness. Who Can Apply? Getting a Hustler Fund loan is easy, but you need to meet a few basic requirements: Be a Kenyan citizen 18 years or older. Have a valid national ID. Be registered on M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or T-Kash. Have an active SIM card for at least 90 days. That&rsquo;s it! No complicated forms, no long lines, and no unnecessary stress. How to Apply (It’s Super Simple!) Dial the USSD code (*254# or the official Hustler Fund code). Choose the loan request option and enter the amount you need. Wait for instant approval&mdash;you&rsquo;ll get an answer within seconds. If approved, the money is sent directly to your mobile wallet (M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or T-Kash). You&rsquo;re good to go! Use the cash for whatever you need&mdash;business or personal use. How to Repay and What Happens If You Delay You have 14 days to pay back at 8% interest. If you pay late by over 14 days from the day of taking the loan, the interest rate will go up to 9.5% per year. Timely payments increase your loan limit, meaning you can borrow a higher limit and be eligible to other Hustler Fund products like Bridge loan So, paying on time keeps you stress-free and unlock bigger loan amounts in the future. Why Kenyans Love the Hustler Fund Since launching, the Hustler Fund Personal Loan has helped millions of Kenyans by: Providing instant cash to boost small businesses. Encouraging saving with automatic savings deductions. Keeping interest rates low, unlike shylocks and expensive mobile loan apps. Improving financial inclusion, making loans available to those locked out by traditional banks. Final Thoughts The Hustler Fund Personal Loan is a game-changer for Kenyans who need quick, affordable, and accessible loans. It&rsquo;s simple to apply, doesn&rsquo;t need collateral, and even helps you save as you borrow. So, if you ever need a quick financial boost, why not give the Hustler Fund Personal Loan a shot?
